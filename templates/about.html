About Tommy Timecrunch

Revolutionizing hackathon management with powerful tools for organizers and participants

Our Mission

Tommy Timecrunch was created with a singular purpose: to simplify the complexities of organizing hackathons and make participation seamless.

We understand the challenges that come with coordinating large-scale technical events. From participant registration to travel logistics, housing arrangements, and project submissions - we've built a comprehensive platform that handles it all.

Our goal is to let organizers focus on creating amazing experiences while participants can concentrate on what they do best - building innovative solutions.
